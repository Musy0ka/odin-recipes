Mango-Peached Smoothie Recipe

A smoothie to go with the hot weather

You can use fresh or frozen fruit in this yummy mango and peach smoothie. It tastes better if the fruit is frozen, so I freeze fresh fruit for this.

Ingredients

  • 1 peach, sliced
  • 1 mango, peeled and diced
  • ½ cup vanilla soy milk
  • ½ cup orange juice, or as needed

Steps

  1. Place peach, mango, soy milk, and orange juice into a blender. Cover, and puree until smooth. Pour into glasses to serve
